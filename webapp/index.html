My cTrader Bot

Advanced Market Analysis for cTrader Platform

Our Telegram-based application provides real-time technical analysis for Forex pairs within the cTrader ecosystem. By leveraging a combination of technical indicators and multi-timeframe analysis (MTA), our bot helps traders identify potential market opportunities and make more informed decisions.

Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Our application uses standard Telegram user IDs for the sole purpose of personalizing the user experience (e.g., managing a watchlist). We do not collect, store, or share any personal data beyond what is necessary for the bot's core functionality. All cTrader API credentials and access tokens are managed securely on the server-side and are never exposed to the client.

Status: The application is currently in the final stages of integration with the cTrader Open API.
